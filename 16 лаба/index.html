Simple tips to maintain your health

  1. Eat a healthy and balanced diet
  2. Keep your brain active and healthy
  3. Drink plenty of water
  4. Improve sleep quality
  5. Manage and reduce stress

Habits of productive people

  1. Make a reasonable to-do list
  2. Take more breaks
  3. Use your morning to focus on yourself
  4. Stop multi-tasking
  5. Find time to walk or exercise

Tips to help manage and reduce your stress levels

  1. Avoid caffeine,alcohol and nicotine
  2. Manage your time
  3. Try relaxation techniques
  4. Rest if you are ill
  5. Learn to say ‘No’
